Welcome to Your Medico RFID

RFID is a technology which uses a certain range of frequency (in waves) in order to identify the presence of some item. RFID technology has been widely deployed in many industries including medical, due the value it adds to the business. RFID consists of two physical components, a tag (which attaches to an object) and a reader. Once the reader has sensed the tag, it will update the database so that hospital management can stay informed.
